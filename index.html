No child should ever be lead poisoned. The Lead Safe Cleveland Coalition is working to make housing lead safe for everyone in Cleveland.

All homes built before 1978 used lead paint, so the City of Cleveland is requiring that all rental properties built before 1978 have a Lead Safe Certification. This certification ensures that homes are lead safe. Starting March 2021, phases of zip code groups will be required to get the certification.


The Lead Safe Cleveland Data Dashboard is designed to give you quick answers to questions you may have about the rollout and impact of the Cleveland Lead Safe Ordinance.
