We believe that no child should ever be lead poisoned. That is why we’re working with XXX organizations to make housing lead safe for everyone in Cleveland. All homes built before 1978 used lead paint, so the City of Cleveland is requiring that all rental properties built before 1978 have a Lead Safe Certification. This certification ensures that homes are lead safe. Starting March 2021, phases of zip code groups will be required to get the certification.
